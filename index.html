ABOUT

I graduated from the University of Georgia (Go Dawgs!) in May 2019 with a Bachelors of Science
in Fashion Merchandising and a minor in Studio Art. My passion for art has been cultivating for as
long as I can remember; I started coding when I was 12 and have never looked back.

When I was 17 years old, I started a photography business that has now evolved into a creative
outlet for me. So what's changed? Since then, I have expanded my business to include most creative
services. From illustration and iconography to cross-app implementation and back-end web design.
I have gone from using my artistic eye for photo composition, to fully implementing web applications.
My keen artistic eye helps me make beautiful applications while still keeping UI/UX and responsivity
in mind. I continue to expand on my language knowledge, and as time goes forward my craft has
cultivated.

Please feel free to see my work below, and reach out via the contact section if you're interested in working together!
